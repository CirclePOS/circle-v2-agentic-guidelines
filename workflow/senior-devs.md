## Approval signal

The senior dev applies one of two labels on the Story:

- **`IP-Pass`** — plan approved; developer proceeds to implementation.
- **`IP-Fail`** — plan needs revision; developer updates the IP, posts a new comment with the revised plan, re-tags the senior dev, and removes the `IP-Fail` label to restart review.

Developer never applies these labels themselves — only senior devs do.
